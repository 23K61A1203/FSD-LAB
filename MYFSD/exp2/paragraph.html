Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam (1931–2015), known as the "Missile Man of India," was a renowned aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India (2002–2007). He played a pivotal role in India's space and missile development programs. His life and teachings continue to inspire millions of students and professionals across the nation. He believed strongly in the power of youth and education.
